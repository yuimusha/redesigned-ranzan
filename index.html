Authentic Japanese Cuisine

At Ran Zan, we pamper you with traditional Japanese hospitality while you enjoy our extraordinary food. Whether you've experienced Japanese cuisine in Boston or Providence Japanese restaurants, we offer you the pearl of Japanese dining.

Our Story

After many years of expertly preparing Japanese delicacies for New Englanders, our owner opened Ran Zan in the Fall of 1998. Today, all of us at Ran Zan take pride in bringing you the colors, flavors, and ambiance that are the hallmarks of truly enjoyable Japanese dining. Named after a country village in Japan, Ran Zan brings to New England the authentic tastes of Japan, from scrupulously fresh sushi and sashimi to light tempura specialties, teriyaki, Japanese noodle soups, and Yakisoba stir-fried Japanese noodles offered with a spectrum of delicious accompaniments.

Explore the Taste

All our fish at Ran Zan, must be scrupulously fresh to meet our own standards and your very high expectations. That's why we have established close relationships with top suppliers who we know and trust.

Japanese food is much more than sushi. At Ran Zan, we offer a spectrum of Japanese dishes including soups, a variety of vegetable, meat, and seafood Tempura, teriyaki, and much more. All of them make for outstanding dining.
